## Visual Representation
You can find the detailed graph in [project_graph.md](file:///Users/ff3300/.gemini/antigravity/brain/70b1513e-acfa-42b7-8f01-51e4a3232cf0/project_graph.md).

```mermaid
graph TD
    subgraph Blueprints
        B_Default[default.yml]
        B_Sedi[sedi.yml]

    end

    subgraph Templates
        T_Default[default.php]
        T_Sedi[sedi.php]
        T_CalendarCSV[calendar-from-csv.php]
    end

    subgraph Snippets
        S_Header[header.php]
        S_Menu[menu.php]
        S_Footer[footer.php]
    end

    B_Default --> T_Default
    T_Default --> S_Header
    T_Default --> S_Menu
    T_Default --> S_Footer
```
